# Portal Customization

## Branding

- **Logo** — upload your logo, shown in the portal header
- **Product name** — displayed as the portal title
- **Description** — subtitle text on your portal

## Colors

Customize the accent color to match your brand. The portal adapts its buttons, links, and highlights.

## Layout

Choose what to show:

- **Tags** — show or hide tags on announcement cards
- **Dates** — show or hide published dates
- **Subscribe form** — show or hide the email subscription form

## Analytics

Connect analytics to track portal traffic:

- **Google Analytics** — add your GA4 measurement ID
- **Fathom Analytics** — add your Fathom site ID

Configure under **Settings → Portal → Analytics**.
