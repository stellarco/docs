# Custom Domains

Instead of `yourworkspace.usestellar.com`, use your own domain like `updates.yourcompany.com`.

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings → Portal → Custom Domain**
2. Enter your domain (e.g., `updates.yourcompany.com`)
3. Add the CNAME record to your DNS provider:
   ```
   CNAME  updates.yourcompany.com  →  portal.usestellar.com
   ```
4. Click **Verify Domain**
5. SSL is provisioned automatically (takes a few minutes)

## Verification

Stellar checks your DNS configuration and shows the status:

- **Pending** — waiting for DNS propagation
- **Active** — domain verified, SSL active
- **Failed** — DNS record not found (check your configuration)

## Removing a custom domain

Remove the custom domain anytime. Your portal reverts to `yourworkspace.usestellar.com`.
