# Billing

Admins manage billing under **Settings → Billing**.

## Plans

View available plans and your current subscription. Upgrade or downgrade at any time — changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Payment methods

Add or update your credit card. Stellar uses Stripe for payment processing.

## Invoices

View and download past invoices from the billing page.

## Cancellation

Cancel your subscription from the billing page. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
