# Profile

Your profile settings live under **Settings → Profile**.

## What you can update

- **Name** — how you appear to teammates
- **Avatar** — upload a photo
- **Email** — your login email
- **Password** — change your password

## Role

Your role is shown on your profile but can only be changed by an admin.
