# Notifications

Stellar notifies you about activity that's relevant to your work.

## What triggers notifications

- Someone assigns a feature to you
- Someone comments on a feature you own or follow
- Someone @mentions you
- A feature you follow changes status

## Notification settings

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to control:

- **Email notifications** — on/off per notification type
- **In-app notifications** — always on, but you can mute specific types

## Following

Follow any feature or insight to get notified about changes. Click the follow icon on the detail view. Unfollow anytime.

## Mark as read

Click a notification to mark it read. Use **Mark all as read** to clear the list.
