# Sign Up

1. Go to [usestellar.com/signup](https://usestellar.com/signup)
2. Enter your email and password, or sign up with Google
3. Confirm your email
4. You're in

After signup, Stellar walks you through creating your first project.

## Invitations

If a teammate invited you, click the link in your invitation email. You'll join their workspace automatically with the role they assigned (admin, maker, or contributor).
