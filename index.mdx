## What is Stellar?

Stellar helps product teams capture, organize, and connect customer feedback to drive better product decisions. Stop losing insights in Slack threads and support tickets - build what users actually need.

## Key Features

### For Product Managers
- **Unified Inbox** - All feedback in one searchable place
- **Feature Linking** - Connect insights directly to roadmap items
- **AI Summaries** - Understand patterns across hundreds of insights
- **Priority Scoring** - Data-driven feature prioritization

### For Customer Teams
- **Chrome Extension** - Capture feedback without leaving your workflow
- **Email Forwarding** - Send feedback directly to Stellar
- **Slack Integration** - Turn conversations into insights
- **Auto-categorization** - AI-powered organization

### For Engineering
- **GitHub Sync** - Two-way feature status updates
- **API Access** - Build custom integrations
- **Webhooks** - Real-time notifications
- **Views** - Custom filters for your workflow
